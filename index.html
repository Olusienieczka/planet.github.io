about the planet

  • The planet is Earth-like with a radius of 8919.4 km.
  • The mass of the planet is 1.4 times the mass of the Earth.
  • The star of this planet is a yellow dwarf, similar in size to the Sun.
  • The average distance of the planet from the star is about 174 million km.

Moons of KB/2c

Lurius

Lurius is azure and 1.5 times larger than our moon. The azure moon has deposits of azurite mixed with calcium and quartz.

Rubrium

Rubrium is pale red and it is 2 times larger than Earth's moon.

physical conditions

  • The planet's gravity is about 11 m/s².
  • The period of the planet's rotation around its axis there lasts 72 Earth hours.
  • The planet's full rotation around the sun takes 373 days.
  • The atmosphere consists of 31% oxygen, 65% nitrogen, and the remaining 4% are noble and greenhouse gases.
  • Water occupies 46% of the planet's surface, and the rest is land. About 80% of the planet's water is drinkable.

geography and hydrosphere

  • The planet has nine continents.
  • There are 3 oceans and 9 seas on its surface.
  • Water on the planet is not salty.

nature/Life on the planet

  • There are more dangerous bacteria and viruses than on Earth, but there are far fewer of them.
  • All organisms on the planet are more developed and have more sensitive senses.
  • There is a preponderance of herbivores on the planet.

climates on the planet

  • The planet has three climates: equatorial, polar and temperate.
  • In the polar climate, plants have very long roots to draw heat from the depths and distribute it throughout the plant. The vegetation is dense and diverse, and the average temperature is around -35 degrees Celsius.
  • The equatorial climate is the most diverse of all, inhabited by 60% of all animal species found on the planet, and the average temperature is 26 degrees Celsius. It is a very humid climate.
  • The temperate climate has very fertile soils, and the planet is rich in resources found at shallow depths.
